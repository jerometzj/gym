About Clementi ActiveSG

Clementi ActiveSG Gym, formerly known as Clementi ClubFITT Gym, is a public gym operated by Sport Singapore.

With a busy interchange and train station nearby, Clementi Sport Centre, formerly known as Clementi Sport and Recreation Centre, serves a mature estate with people of different ages, from kids, young adults, to the active seniors. Clementi is the preferred venue for Ministry of Education's basketball championships and is training ground for our Singapore fencing team.

Though it is one of the oldest facility, Clementi has been able to re-invent itself through bringing amateur boxing to the masses to revive the interest of the working adults.

Services

Equipped with equipments for every exercise, Clementi ActiveSG ensures that you will have an effective time working out.

There are three sections to the gym. A Free weights area where, a stretching area for cool down or warm up, and a cardiovascular area where you can find treadmills and cycling stations .
